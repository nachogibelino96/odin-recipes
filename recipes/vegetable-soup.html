Vegetable soup

This Soup has become one of my most popular soup recipes and for good reason! It’s healthy, it’s comforting and 1,000 times better than what you’ll get in a can! Full of flavor and so easy to make you can’t go wrong with a big warm bowl of vegetable soup.

But...What will you need to get it? Here we go:

  • 2 Carrots.
    • No more, no less...
  • 1 Potatoe.
  • 2 Tomatoes.
    • Please, try to get some red tomatoes.
  • 1 Yellow Onion.
  • ...and finally, 1 garlick.
    •

Now we have all we need, let's start that cooking!

  1. Start cutting all the vegetables. Don't cut your fingers, if you do so, your soup will turn into a meat and vegetable soup.
  2. Put all what you cut in a hot water.
  3. Enjoy yourself!
